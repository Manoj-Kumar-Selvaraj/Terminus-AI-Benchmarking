Extend the booking adjustment reconciler in `/app/cmd/reconcile/main.go` to handle dated adjustment batches. It must still read `/app/data/bookings.csv` and `/app/data/adjustments.csv`, then write `/app/out/adjustment_report.csv` and `/app/out/adjustment_summary.json` with the same schema and status values from the earlier milestones. `bookings.csv` may include a `due_date` column and `adjustments.csv` may include a `adjustment_date` column. A adjustment can match only when all prior criteria still pass, the adjustment date is listed as `open` in `/app/config/cutoff_calendar.txt`, and the adjustment date is not later than the booking due date. A missing or closed adjustment date is not eligible. A booking with a missing due date is also not eligible because the adjustment-date comparison cannot be satisfied. If more than one unused booking matches the same adjustment, choose the eligible booking with the latest due date; if due dates tie, choose the earliest booking row. Each booking can still be consumed at most once. Legacy aliases from milestone 2 still apply (`CC` means `CARD`, `WIR` means `WIRE`), and matched report rows must emit the canonical channel. Unmatched rows must leave `channel` blank. Summary amounts remain positive integer cents.
