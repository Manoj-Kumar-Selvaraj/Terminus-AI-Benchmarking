Extend the childcare refund reconciliation CLI to support method and settlement eligibility. It must still build from `/app/cmd/reconcile`, read `/app/data/sessions.csv` and `/app/data/refunds.csv`, use runtime configs under `/app/config`, and regenerate `/app/out/refund_report.csv` and `/app/out/refund_summary.json` with the same schemas and status labels.

All earlier rules still apply: header-addressed CSV parsing, positive integer amounts, full `session_id` equality, `guardian_id` equality, `CHECKEDIN` session status, runtime room aliases and canonical room output, single-use session rows, open `refund_date` from `/app/config/cutoff_calendar.txt`, `refund_date` not later than `attendance_date`, and latest-attendance-date selection with earliest-row tie-breaking.

For this milestone, `refunds.csv` may include `refund_method` and may include `settlement_date`. The `refund_method` column is input-only; do not add it to the report or summary. When `refund_method` is present, a refund can match only if the trimmed, case-folded method is enabled in `/app/config/methods.csv`. The methods config is header-addressed, may include extra columns, and has required columns `method`, `enabled`, and `max_lag_days`. Enabled accepts `true`, `y`, `yes`, or `1` after trim and case folding. Blank, disabled, malformed, or unlisted methods are ineligible. If a method appears more than once, the first row for that method wins and later duplicate rows are ignored, even when the first row is disabled or malformed. `max_lag_days` must be a non-negative integer.

When `settlement_date` is present in `refunds.csv`, it must be a strict `YYYY-MM-DD` date, it must not be before `refund_date`, and it must be no more than the method's `max_lag_days` calendar days after `refund_date`, inclusive. Missing, blank, malformed, too-early, or too-late settlement dates make the refund ineligible. If `refund_method` is absent, preserve the previous dated matching behavior and do not apply the method gate. If `settlement_date` is absent, apply only the method gate and skip the settlement lag check.
