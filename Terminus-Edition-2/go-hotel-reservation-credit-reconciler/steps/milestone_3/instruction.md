Extend the reservation credit reconciler in `/app/cmd/reconcile/main.go` to handle dated credit batches. It must still read `/app/data/reservations.csv` and `/app/data/credits.csv`, then write `/app/out/credit_report.csv` and `/app/out/credit_summary.json` with the same schema and status values from the earlier milestones. `reservations.csv` may include a `due_date` column and `credits.csv` may include a `credit_date` column. A credit can match only when all prior criteria still pass, the credit date is listed as `open` in `/app/config/cutoff_calendar.txt` (case-insensitive, so `open` and `OPEN` are both valid), and the credit date is not later than the reservation due date. A missing or closed credit date is not eligible. A reservation with a missing due date is also not eligible because the credit-date comparison cannot be satisfied. If more than one unused reservation matches the same credit, choose the eligible reservation with the latest due date; if due dates tie, choose the earliest reservation row. Reservation consumption is tracked by input row position, not by reservation id, so duplicate reservation ids in separate rows remain independently consumable until their specific rows are used. Legacy aliases from milestone 2 still apply (`CC` means `CARD`, `WIR` means `WIRE`), and matched report rows must emit the canonical channel. Unmatched rows must leave `channel` blank. Summary amounts remain positive integer cents.
