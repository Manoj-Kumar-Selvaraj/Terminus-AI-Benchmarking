Each claim can be consumed by at most one credit, with the earliest eligible credit winning. Header-only `claims.csv` or `credits.csv` inputs from milestone 1 still apply: an empty credit file yields only the report header and zero summary totals, while credits against a claims file with only a header remain unmatched with positive unmatched totals. Report `claim_id`, `patient_id`, and `amount_cents` must not carry incidental surrounding spaces from credit input. The summary JSON at `/app/out/credit_summary.json` must contain only integer-typed fields `matched_count`, `matched_amount_cents`, `unmatched_count`, and `unmatched_amount_cents` (not strings or floats), with credit amounts counted as positive cents.
