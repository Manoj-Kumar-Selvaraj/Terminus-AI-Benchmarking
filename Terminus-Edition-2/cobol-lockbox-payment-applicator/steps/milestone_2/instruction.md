Extend the lockbox applicator in `/app/src/lockbox_apply.cbl` for a legacy payment feed. It must still read `/app/data/invoices.dat` and `/app/data/payments.dat`, then write `/app/out/lockbox_report.csv` and `/app/out/lockbox_summary.txt`. A payment applies only when the full 12-character invoice id, customer id, amount, open invoice status, invoice hold flag `N`, payment disposition `P`, payment date not later than the invoice cutoff date, and allowed channel all line up. Channels `ACH`, `WIR`, `CRD`, and `LBX` are allowed.

The new legacy feed uses payment-channel aliases: `LKB` means `LBX`, `BNK` means `ACH`, and `CCP` means `CRD`. Treat those aliases as their canonical channels for matching and report output, while unknown aliases remain unapplied. Each invoice can still be applied at most once, with the earliest eligible payment winning. Keep `/app/out/lockbox_report.csv` in payment input order with columns `invoice_id,customer_id,channel,amount_cents,payment_date,status`; `UNAPPLIED` rows must leave `channel` blank. The summary must use `key=value` lines for `applied_count`, `applied_amount_cents`, `unapplied_count`, and `unapplied_amount_cents`, with payment amounts counted as positive cents.
