Parallel backlog draining exposed two linked failures: competing workers selected the same live delivery, and a delayed older generation remained retryable after a newer generation was already active. Review `/app/evidence/dual_worker_trace.log`, `/app/evidence/stale_ack_trace.log`, `/app/docs/concurrency_contract.md`, and `/app/docs/delivery_contract.md`.

Make shared-state dispatch safe across controller processes and worker goroutines. Claims must obey the documented 30-second logical lease, expired work must be takeable with the same command identity and a newer claim token, and completion from obsolete ownership must not overwrite current state. Newer generations may progress while older requests are in flight, but delayed or locally obsolete generations must finish as `superseded` without regressing gateway state or generating avoidable requests. Keep concurrency enabled and preserve the public CLI, status schema, journal, failpoints, and gateway protocol.
