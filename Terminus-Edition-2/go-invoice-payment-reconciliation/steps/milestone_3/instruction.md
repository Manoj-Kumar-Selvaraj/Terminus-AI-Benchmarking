Milestone 1 and milestone 2 behavior must remain correct while adding date controls. Invoices may include a `due_date` column after `method`, and payments may include a `payment_date` column after `method`; rows without the required date column are readable but ineligible to match in milestone 3. Keep the deliverable as a Go CLI compiled with `/usr/local/go/bin/go`.

Use `/app/config/cutoff_calendar.txt`, where each non-blank line has a date and status separated by whitespace. Calendar status matching is case-insensitive. Only dates whose last authoritative row is `open` are valid payment dates; duplicate calendar rows for the same date use the last row. Dates missing from the calendar, blank dates, malformed dates, closed dates, malformed `due_date`, and malformed `payment_date` values are ineligible and must not crash the CLI.

A payment matches only when all prior criteria still hold, the payment date is open, and the payment date is not later than the invoice due date. If multiple unconsumed invoices satisfy all criteria for one payment, choose the invoice with the latest eligible `due_date`; when `due_date` ties, choose the earliest invoice input row. Keep `/app/out/payment_report.csv` and `/app/out/payment_summary.json` schemas unchanged.
